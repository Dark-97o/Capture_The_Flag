ACM CTF CYBER CHALLENGE

Advanced Cybersecurity Training & Random Data Analysis

Mission Briefing

Welcome, cyber operative! Your mission is to infiltrate this data analysis system and extract the hidden flag. Use your skills in web reconnaissance, source code analysis, and pattern recognition. The flag is distributed across multiple locations - piece it together to complete your mission.

Progress: Mission Started - Begin Investigation

Challenge Rules & Information

  • Objective: Find and assemble the hidden flag using web reconnaissance, source code analysis, and pattern recognition.
  • Team Composition: Individual participation or teams of 1–2 members are allowed. (All participants must be registered.)
  • Time Limit: The CTF will run for 3 hours (9:30 AM – 12:30 PM). Keep track of your progress carefully.
  • Submission: A Google submission form will be provided at 12:00 PM and must be completed before 12:30 PM. Late submissions will not be accepted.
  • Instructions: Explore websites, source code, and hidden patterns to uncover flag fragments. Check browser console, source code comments, and network responses for clues. Submit your final assembled flag via the provided submission form.
  • Flag Distribution: The main flag is divided into 3 parts. When combined, they provide the link to the next webpage.
  • Bonus Flag: A unique bonus key is hidden across all 10 webpages. Collecting all its fragments unlocks a 5-point bonus flag.
    • The number of bonus key fragments is unknown and may vary.
    • Bonus flag will only be considered if fully completed.
  • Flag Format: Flag fragments will follow this format → Secret Key Part 1/2/3 : xyzabc
  • Rules: No brute-forcing, no denial-of-service attacks, and no sharing of solutions. Any violation = immediate disqualification.
